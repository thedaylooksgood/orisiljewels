# ROSE GOLD ELEGANCE — MASTER DESIGN SYSTEM

## PROJECT OVERVIEW

Create a premium luxury jewelry e-commerce website.

The website must feel:

* Elegant
* Feminine
* Luxurious
* Soft
* Editorial
* Timeless
* High-end
* Modern Luxury

The design should feel closer to a luxury fashion house than a typical Shopify jewelry store.

Avoid looking like:

* Generic e-commerce templates
* Dark luxury websites
* Corporate websites
* Tech startups
* Fashion marketplaces

Inspiration:
Luxury jewelry boutiques, premium bridal brands, luxury editorial magazines, modern fashion houses.

---

# DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Every component must follow these principles:

1. Soft visual hierarchy
2. Plenty of whitespace
3. Large elegant typography
4. High quality jewelry photography
5. Soft shadows only
6. Rounded corners
7. Subtle animations
8. Consistent spacing
9. Premium micro-interactions
10. Clean luxury aesthetic

The experience should feel expensive, romantic and sophisticated.
